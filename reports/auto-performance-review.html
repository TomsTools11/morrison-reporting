06

Recommendations

1
Direct the increased budget to proven sources
Premium Referral produced the most leads, the most quotes, and both bound policies; Email and the Premium Paid Search sources are converting leads to quotes at low cost. We are applying the June 3 budget increase to these sources and holding Premium Referral's placement.
2
Resolve Native/Display/Social delivery
This source holds the largest share of available auctions but is winning no ad placements at its current bid. We are reviewing it with the platform team to restore delivery or reallocate that demand to converting sources. This is where the added Auto budget capacity currently sits.
3
Weight bids toward multi-vehicle households
On June 18 we raised the multi-vehicle modifier to grow the share of multi-car leads, currently 29% of Auto volume and the agency's stronger-closing segment.
4
Work every lead on the 30-day follow-up cadence
Our recommended 30-day, 60+ touch cadence across phone, text, and email: GOAL 30-Day Lead Follow-Up Playbook →
