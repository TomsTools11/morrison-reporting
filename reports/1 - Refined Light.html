04

Your 30-day growth plan

The same playbook top-performing agencies use to convert exclusive leads — built on one fact: the leads that close are reached first and most often.

1
Respond within 5 minutes
A lead contacted within 5 minutes is dramatically more likely to pick up than one called an hour later. When a GOAL lead comes in, it's the highest-priority task in your day. A quick text right after a missed call lifts callback rates.
2
Work every lead across 30 days, not 2 calls
Most agents stop after two or three attempts — often right before the lead would have answered. A structured cadence over 30 days separates a 2-3% close rate from a 10%+ close rate.
