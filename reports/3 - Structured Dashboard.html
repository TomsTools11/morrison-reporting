The leads that close are reached first and most often

Respond within 5 minutes
A lead contacted within 5 minutes is dramatically more likely to pick up than one called an hour later. When a GOAL lead comes in, it's the highest-priority task in your day. A quick text after a missed call lifts callback rates.

Most agents stop after two or three attempts — often right before the lead would have answered. A structured cadence separates a 2-3% close rate from a 10%+ close rate.
