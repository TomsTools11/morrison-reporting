06

Recommendations

1
Investigate and re-bid Tier 1
Tier 1 is the largest pool of available Home leads (18,000+ opportunities) but is winning about 0.5% of auctions at its current bid. It has produced the highest-quality leads and the bound policy. We are confirming delivery with the platform team and raising the bid into the in-market competitive range.
2
Exclude existing Allstate customers
On June 18 we tightened targeting to filter out existing Allstate customers. Home carried the majority of these: 34 of 54 flagged in Lead Manager since launch. We are monitoring the effect through June 24.
3
Prioritize single-family homes
On June 18 we raised the Single Family Home modifier and reduced bids on renter property types (apartment, condominium, duplex, townhome). Single-family homes account for 71 of 79 leads, 11 of 12 quotes, and the bound policy.
4
Work every lead on the 30-day follow-up cadence
Our recommended 30-day, 60+ touch cadence across phone, text, and email: GOAL 30-Day Lead Follow-Up Playbook →
5
Hold Tier 3
Tier 3 is the primary source of Home leads and quotes this period. Placement and volume are steady; we are leaving it unchanged.
6
Maintain the reduced bid on Home Search
Market clearing prices for this source run well above the rest of the campaign. Budget is better deployed on Tier 1 through Tier 3.
